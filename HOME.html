About Nelson Mandela

Nelson Mandela was a South African anti-apartheid revolutionary, political leader, and philanthropist who became the country’s first black president from 1994 to 1999. Imprisoned for 27 years due to his resistance against apartheid, Mandela is celebrated for his commitment to peace, justice, and reconciliation. His leadership helped dismantle apartheid, bringing democracy to South Africa. Mandela’s legacy is one of courage, forgiveness, and unwavering dedication to human rights and equality
